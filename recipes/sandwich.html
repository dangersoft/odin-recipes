Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich

A delicious and simple snack for any time of the day.

The peanut butter and jelly sandwich is a classic treat, eaten most often at lunch, after exercising, and at 2am while drunk. It combines the essential snacky crunch of toast with the richness of peanut butter and sweet tang of jelly. There's also a secret 4th ingredient we'll reveal below!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread (recommended: whole grain)
  • peanut butter
  • fruit jelly (traditionally grape but can be any)

Steps

  1. Toast both slices of bread to liking.
  2. Spread layer of peanut butter on one slice (thickness to liking).
  3. Spread layer of jelly on OTHER slice (thickness to liking).
  4. Gently press slices together with peanut butter and jelly slices facing each other.
  5. Serve on plate!
