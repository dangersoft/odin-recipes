Good Pizza

The tastiest pizza is all the land.

If you know pizza, you know pizza is life. It is the sun, moon, water, skies, earth, and trees.

Pizza is the past, present, and future. If you live by the Pizza Code, someday you'll be able to join the Great Big Pizza Pie in the Sky.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Everything else needed to make the dough
  • Mozzarella (and any other cheese you want)
  • BBQ sauce
  • Lil chicken chunks
  • Red bell peppers (chopped)

Steps

  1. Preheat your brick oven to 1000° F.
  2. Take that flour and other stuff and make that delicious, sticky dough.
  3. Stretch the dough out into a classic pizza pie shape
  4. Spread the BBQ sauce out to liking
  5. Add all the toppings (chicken chunks, cheese(s), chopped red bell peppers.
  6. Bake until cheese is golden brown (10-45 min)
  7. Let cool for 5 minutes, cut into smaller slices, and serve on plates!
